Frequently Asked

Common questions about our service

Do you offer any warranty on accessories? +

Tempered glass and covers are replaced free if defective within 7 days of purchase. Cables, chargers, and earphones carry a 1-month replacement warranty against manufacturing defects.

What's the minimum order for wholesale pricing? +

Wholesale rates apply from 10 pieces per item. Mixing categories across a bigger order can also qualify for extra slab discounts — just ask on WhatsApp.

What payment modes do you accept? +

Cash and UPI at the counter for walk-ins. Wholesale and bulk orders can also be settled via bank transfer.

How do you decide the buyback price for my phone? +

Price depends on brand, model, age, working condition, and whether the box or accessories are included. We quote instantly and pay on the spot once you agree.

Do you buy phones that don't switch on at all? +

Yes. Even non-working or heavily damaged units still have value in parts, so we'll always make you an offer.

How fast do I get paid after selling my phone? +

Same day, in cash, right at the counter — there's no waiting period.
